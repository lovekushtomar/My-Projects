Our Founder



Shradha Khapra

YouTube

Birth and Education:

She was born in Nathurpur village, Haryana. She completed her schooling at Jain Bharati Mrigavati Vidyalaya and earned a Computer Engineering degree from Netaji Subhash Institute of Technology (NSIT).

Career

Shradha worked as a software engineer intern at Microsoft and also did a one-month internship at DRDO. After working at Microsoft for five months, she started her Apna College YouTube channel.

YouTube and Teaching:

She runs Apna College, a popular YouTube channel where she teaches coding and helps aspiring developers.
